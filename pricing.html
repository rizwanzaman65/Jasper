Jasper is an on-brand AI platform for businesses. It helps creators use generative AI to break through writer’s block, create original imagery, and repackage content into different formats, tones and languages. Unlike most AI tools which are tied to individual tools and can produce pretty generic outputs, Jasper can be trained on your brand and called up on any platform you use from email to social media to your website.

Over 100,000 businesses small and large use Jasper to scale up content and rate their experience 4.8/5 stars in over 10k reviews.

Is Jasper for me?

If you're on this page, then chances are someone referred you to Jasper, you Googled something related to AI and Marketing, or you saw an ad that caught your attention... so it's not a coincidence that you're reading this right now.

Jasper is the best AI for businesses because of 4 key differentiators:

Whether you are a social media manager, SEO specialist, ads manager, email marketer, or you're in marketing leadership then Jasper will be you're home-base for creating content.
  2. If staying on-brand is important for you, then Jasper is the only AI solution for you. Other AIs sound generic... like a robot wrote it. Jasper is different. Jasper is a multi-player AI platform where you can assign work, see status updates, and set workspace defaults for AI features like setting your brand-voice, teaching the AI about your products, and setting a language default.

So in summary, Jasper is for you if you want high-performing content written quickly with AI that is factually accurate and always on-brand.
